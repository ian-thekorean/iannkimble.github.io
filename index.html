About

I help revenue teams turn their GTM data into pipeline decisions they can actually act on. That means building the reporting, maintaining the systems, and closing the gaps between what CRMs capture and what leadership needs to see — so that BD, Sales, and CS aren’t flying blind.

My path into RevOps started in customer analytics at a utility company in Louisville, where I learned how to translate operational data into insights that leadership would actually read. From there I moved into BI consulting at MarketStar, building reporting frameworks and Looker Studio dashboards for Google’s Enterprise and Education sales teams. That work sharpened my SQL chops and taught me how to design scalable analytics for fast-moving BDR orgs.

Now at Trella Health, I own the full RevOps stack — Salesforce, Marketo, Planhat, Gong, Salesloft — and build the weekly KPI reporting that BD, Sales, and CS leadership depends on. I’ve compressed sales cycles, overhauled lifecycle campaigns in Marketo end-to-end, and maintained zero-error records across five integrated platforms. I’m most energized when I can connect a dashboard metric to a real decision someone made that week.
